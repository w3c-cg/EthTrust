## Reporting a Vulnerability

We are in the process of setting up a reponsible vulnerability reporting procedure. 
In the meantime, please report urgent problems to a security company who has a member in the group, following their vulnerability reporting procedures.

Please ensure that you specify the problem is with the EthTrust specification, and identify the company's participant in the group to ensure it is brought to their attention.
